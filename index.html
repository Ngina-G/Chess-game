What is Chess?

Chess, one of the oldest and most popular board games, played by two opponents on a checkered board with specially designed pieces of contrasting colours, commonly white and black. White moves first, after which the players alternate turns in accordance with fixed rules, each player attempting to force the opponent’s principal piece, the King, into checkmate—a position where it is unable to avoid capture.

For many beginners, chess seems overly complicated and unnecessarily difficult. Often being passed over for other less mentally taxing taxing board games during game night. With each piece moving in its own unique way, chess can take time to learn and master.

Our product gives people curious about chess, beginners and master who want to refresh their skills the opportunity to understand the way each piece moves. We offer a fun and interactive way to become a Grand-master with patience and practice.
